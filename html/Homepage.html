About Game


Xtream chaser is game about martial arts/fighting. The game is focused on the main character Boy, who competes in a worldwide martial arts tournaments. The player perform some punches, and kick attack, each varying in speed and power, and also there is special attacks: the Stormzys, Whirlwind kick, and One inch punch, performed by executing special button combinations. Gameplay focuses on hand-to-hand combat with an opponent, with the gameplay system including blocks, escapes, and skill like mention above. The Player in a series of one-on-one matches against opponents(BOT) or in a single match against another player. Each match consist of best of 3, which the player must knock out the opponent in less than 30 seconds, if the match ends before the fighter get knock out. the fighter with greater amount of health/energy left will be declared to be the winner of the round. If the third rounds ends in a tie, then the opponent( BOT ) will win by default or both player will lose.
