The Beginning


Ryo is a man with a great attitude and ambitions. He got accident from martial arts and he got and now he left with no memory of his parents. Ryo was raised by elder man with great skill martial arts. He got goals that he want to beat his master and be the best martial arts ever. Everyday he trains as hard as he could. With his ambition and great goals, he believe he will achieve it someday. Throughout most of his life, Ryo trains at his dojo and trained by adoptive father(elder man). He felt he can no longer grow on the dojo, he believe that he got all the experience at the dojo and decide to left the dojo and continually wandered across the globe always seeking to prove himself that he is the best martial arts and wanting to meet new people and fight them.

The World Of Martial Arts


Someday, there is big competition in fighting scene, and it is called The World Martial Arts, and Ryo decided to joined the competition with the ambition: to beat all of the fighter and prove the world that he is the best martial arts ever. After many of matches that he conquer, there are four fighters that remain after winning death matches all over the world. The winner of the tournament will receive the title of worldwide martial artist, and some big prize money. Only one will have a chance to claim the prize money and the fame. Ryo will have to defeat them all to achieve his dream. Will he achieve the dream he ever wanted?
